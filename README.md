What Is Included Inside DFY Video Agency?

The DFY Video Agency package includes three main software applications that help users create engaging video content.

Vidictive

Vidictive gives users access to over 250 high-converting video ad templates optimized for social media marketing.

The drag-and-drop editor makes it easy to customize videos and create attractive promotional content quickly.

Storie

Storie allows users to create short-form video content suitable for platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

The tool is designed to increase engagement through visually appealing video content.

Videlligence

Videlligence uses artificial intelligence technology to turn product pages into ready-made video advertisements automatically.

Simply paste a product link from platforms such as Shopify, Amazon, eBay, or Etsy, and the software collects product information, images, and pricing details to create a professional video ad.
